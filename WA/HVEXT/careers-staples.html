Field Career Opportunities

We are Wireless Advocates, a fast-growing, third-party retailer supporting leading national retailers like Staples - both in stores and online. We specialize in selling high-quality wireless and related technology products and services from major carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, and Verizon Wireless. We are committed to delivering high-value solutions that enrich people's lives by helping them connect. With an expert team of sales professionals, collaborative relationships with leading wireless companies, and a desire to provide superior customer service, we continue to meet and exceed the needs of our customers across the nation.

As part of our dynamic team, you'll have the opportunity to sharpen your sales techniques and learn all about the latest and greatest technology - all while having fun at work!
