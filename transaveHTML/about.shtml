Company Overview

Transave Inc. is an early stage biopharmaceutical company focused on the development of innovative, inhaled product lines for the site-specific treatment of lung diseases. We are committed to significantly improving the quality of life and healthcare of patients suffering from life threatening lung diseases. Our business principles are centered on teamwork, trust, mutual respect and innovative science for all of our employees and partners.

Sample Secondary Heading

The product pipeline is based on Transave’s proprietary Sustained-release Lipid Inhaled Targeting (“SLIT™”) technology. The initial focus is the reformulation off-patent pharmaceuticals transformed into unique SLIT™-based inhaled pharmaceuticals for the localized, site specific treatment of lung disease.

Sample Tertiary Heading

SLIT™ technology allows sustained release of the drug in the lung’s microenvironment resulting in enhanced therapeutic benefits and improved dosing regimens.

Transave’s approach significantly reduces risk and enhances success in drug development by:

  1. avoiding the inherent uncertainties of new chemical entities (“NCEs”);
  2. building upon the demonstrated efficacy of approved agents in the target diseases;
  3. via localized administration and controlled release focuses on reducing or eliminating the systemic known toxicities or dosing problems associated with systemically administered drug, and;
  4. creates a new, improved product profile of a known, well characterized drug.
