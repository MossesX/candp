Benefits

In addition to an outstanding work environment Transave provides a comprehensive and competitive total compensation package for employees and their families. This package includes:

  • Competitive Salary
  • Stock Options
  • Health Insurance
  • Dental Insurance
  • Short-Term/Long-Term Disability and Life Insurance
  • Flexible Spending Accounts for Dependent and Medical Care
  • 401K Plan
  • Paid Vacations and Paid Holidays
  • Business Casual Environment
  • Company Subsidized Cafeteria
  • Company Sponsored Outings and Family Events

Transave reserves the right to amend or change our benefit programs at any time.
