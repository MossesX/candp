Financing

  • Transave completed a Series A financing for $3.75 million in August 2000. This round was led by Techno Venture Management (now TVM-Capital) a world leading venture capital investment group in the biotechnology field.
  • The company completed a $14 million Series B financing in December 2002 lead by ABN AMRO and Easton Hunt Capital Partners. The funds from this round of financing enabled Transave to finish GLP toxicology studies on SLIT™ Amikacin and allow the company to take it and SLIT™ Cisplatin through Phase I/IIa clinical studies. The financing was also used to add additional management in the areas of R&D, finance and commercial/business development.
  • Transave recently completed a $35 million Series C Private Financing, lead by Prospect Venture Partners and Fidelity Bioscience Group. Additional investments were made by TVM-Capital and ABN AMRO. This funding is earmarked to advance the development of SLIT™ Amikacin and SLIT™ Cisplatin to completion of Phase II studies and well into Phase III pivotal trials. Additional product pipeline development will be supported by the financing, as well as general corporate operations.
