New York City to Transave:

  • Take Lincoln Tunnel to New Jersey
  • Merge onto I-95 South (NJ Turnpike)
  • Take Exit 9 off NJ Turnpike
  • Go through toll and merge right onto Rt. 18 North
  • Go a short distance and bear right onto U.S. 1 South (just beyond car wash)
  • Continue on Rt. 1 South for about 12 miles
  • Go through the light at Raymond Road (Mobil Station on corner)
  • Once through the light, go a short distance and make a right at sign reading “Princeton Plaza”. This is Deer Park Drive.
  • Go straight to the third driveway, which says 7 Deer Park Drive and make left.
  • Go straight to the last building (11 Deer Park Drive) and make a right.
  • Transave is located in the middle of the building towards the left. Suite 117.

Newark Airport to Transave:

  • Follow signs to Newark International
  • Airport Exit and New Jersey Tpke I-95.
  • Bear RIGHT onto US-1 & 9 Express Lane.
  • Bear RIGHT, merge onto Route 18 [South] Tollbooth.
  • Merge onto I-95 South [New Jersey Tpke]
  • Follow directions from above once on NJ Turnpike South

Directions from Philadelphia and Trenton to Transave:

  • From Philadelphia:Take US-1 North about 19 miles to New Jersey. Alternate take I-95 to US-1 to New Jersey
  • Stay on US-1 North about 16 miles (From Trenton take US-1 North)
  • Pass the Forrestal Center and Ridge Road then proceed ~1.5 miles
  • Bear right at Raymond Road jug handle, just past Mile Marker 17
  • Take Raymond Road across US-1 making a left onto US-1 South
  • Go a short distance and make a right at sign reading “Princeton Plaza”. This is Deer Park Drive.
  • Go straight to the third driveway, which says 7 Deer Park Drive and make left.
  • Go straight to the last building (11 Deer Park Drive) and make a right.
  • Transave is located in the middle of the building towards the left. Suite 117.
